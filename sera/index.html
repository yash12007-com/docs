Sera Antivirus | Overview

Smart Antivirus Protection for your loved ones

Sera Antivirus | Logo

⬇ Download | Releases

Sera Antivirus is a powerful and user-friendly antivirus solution designed to protect your devices from various cyber threats such as malware, trojans, and ransomware. Sera Antivirus uses the smartest algorithm to detect the threads and allow user to select wether the application/software is trusted or not and deleting it. Sera has a built-in files/folders migration system to migrate/move all files and folders selected by the user to the specified path/location according to user preferences. Each migration can be customized to meet the specific needs of the user and takes minimum time to migrate by removing unnecessary tasks and uses. The most useful function in Sera is the Device optimisation, Sera Antivirus has a feature called device optimisation that removes all unnecessary cached data and optimizes the performance of your device by removing temporary files and turning off or restart all current processes without shutting down the device.

The feature Battery monitor and data monitor are usual features in Sera Antivirus, with minimal processes to check instead of running a full diagnosis. This feature uses very adaptive memory to keep your device calm while performing these actions without opening any other apps until the other tasks in Sera Antivirus are complete.

Sera Antivirus is developed by Yash Bodhe, Programmer, entrepreneur, and founder Yash12007. The rights of the Project Sera are given to the organisation Yash12007 by Yash Bodhe. The software Sera Antivirus comes with an installer (dedicated system installer) to install on your device in your desired language. The software does support only English (EN). The approximate size of Sera Antivirus is 170 Megabytes (170 MB). The software has license given by the developer, programmer, and founder of Yash12007 is propriatory.
